Fennel Orange Salad

Description

This salad features fresh, spring ingredients that are sure to liven up any meal. The orange and fennel pair beautifully with the arugula which adds a bit of bite to the salad. Serve it by itself or as an accompanyment at your next event to wow your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag organic arugula
  • 3 large navel oranges
  • 1/2 head of fennel
  • 2 Tbsp organic EVOO
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste
