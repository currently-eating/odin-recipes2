Fennel Orange Salad

Description

This salad features fresh, spring ingredients that are sure to liven up any meal. The orange and fennel pair beautifully with the arugula which adds a bit of bite to the salad. Serve it by itself or as an accompanyment at your next event to wow your guests.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bag organic arugula
  • 3 large navel oranges
  • 1/2 head of fennel
  • 2 Tbsp organic EVOO
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Empty bag of arugula into large salad bowl.
  2. Using a paring knife, supreme orange slices from all 3 navel oranges. Save the fruit pulp for later.
  3. Using a vegetable pealer, peal slices of fennel until entire half of fennel head is used up.
  4. Add fennel and oranges to salad bowl.
  5. Drizzle organic EVOO onto salad.
  6. Squeeze remnants of orange juice from the left over pulp onto the salad.
  7. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Toss salad and enjoy!
