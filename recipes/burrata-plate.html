Burrata Tomato Plate

Description

This dish is the perfect starter for a summer lunch outside. It features fresh heirloom cherry tomatoes, basil, good quality olive oil, balsamic glaze, and of course, fresh burrata. Paired alongside garlic-rubbed grilled sourdough, it is the perfect way to begin a meal with friends.

Ingredients:

  • 1 fresh burrata ball
  • 1/2 carton organic cherry heirloom tomatoes
  • 5-10 leaves organic basil
  • balsamic glaze
  • good quality organiv EVOO
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • sourdough bread
  • salt and pepper
