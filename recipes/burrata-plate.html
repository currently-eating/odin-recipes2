Burrata Tomato Plate

Description

This dish is the perfect starter for a summer lunch outside. It features fresh heirloom cherry tomatoes, basil, good quality olive oil, balsamic glaze, and of course, fresh burrata. Paired alongside garlic-rubbed grilled sourdough, it is the perfect way to begin a meal with friends.

Ingredients:

  • 1 fresh burrata ball
  • 1/2 carton organic cherry heirloom tomatoes
  • 5-10 leaves organic basil
  • balsamic glaze
  • good quality organiv EVOO
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • sourdough bread
  • salt and pepper

Steps:

  1. Place burrata on plate and spread open.
  2. Slice tomatoes and place on top of burrata.
  3. Drizzle balsamic glaze and EVOO over top.
  4. Stack basil leaves on each other, roll up, and slice into thin strips. Add to dish.
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Toast up slices of sourdough then rub garlic cloves on one side.
  7. Plate toast and serve!
