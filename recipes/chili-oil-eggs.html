Chili Oil Eggs

Description

Chili oil eggs are the perfect way to start the day. A quick but delicious dish that is sure to leave you wanting seconds. Served on top of rice, these eggs make the perfect soul filling meal.

Ingredients:

  • 2 organic pasture-raised eggs
  • 1/2 cup freshly made white rice
  • 1 Tbsp Fly By Jing Sichuan Chili Crisp
  • 1 1/2 Tbsp Sriracha
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2 tsp Furikake
  • 1-2 green onions

Steps:

  1. Place a small pan on the stove and turn heat to medium.
  2. Add Sriracha, chili crisp, grated garlic, and furikake to pan and let it heat up.
  3. Once bubbling a bit, add in the 2 eggs and let cook for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Flip the eggs and let cook for 1 more minute before removing from heat.
  5. Add cooked rice to a bowl or plate and place eggs on top.
  6. Use kitchen scissors to cut the green onion over top the eggs and serve!
